Our Vision

Transforming education through intelligent technology

Opticlass is a smart, AI-powered school management system designed to support CBC in Kenya. It simplifies school administration, empowers teachers with insights, personalizes student learning, and enables parents to be actively involved.

Our platform integrates cutting-edge technology with educational best practices to create a comprehensive ecosystem that supports the unique needs of Kenyan schools and aligns with the Competency-Based Curriculum.

Development Roadmap

A strategic approach to building the Opticlass platform

1MVP (Foundation)

Establishing core functionality

Goals

Build the foundational system with essential school management features

Key Features

  • Student registration and profiles
  • Teacher and staff management
  • Basic timetable functionality
  • Grade and assessment tracking
  • User authentication and roles

Outcome

A solid foundation that streamlines basic administrative tasks and provides a platform for future enhancements.

2Communication & Finance

Enhancing stakeholder engagement

Goals

Improve communication channels and financial management capabilities

Key Features

  • Parent-teacher communication portal
  • Fee management and payment tracking
  • School announcements and notifications
  • Attendance tracking with alerts
  • Financial reporting and analytics

Outcome

Enhanced stakeholder communication and streamlined financial operations with automated reminders and reporting.

3AI & Smart Features

Intelligent insights and personalization

Goals

Implement AI-driven insights and personalized learning experiences

Key Features

  • AI-powered performance analytics
  • Personalized learning paths
  • Predictive analytics for at-risk students
  • Smart timetable optimization
  • Automated report generation

Outcome

Data-driven insights that help identify student needs, optimize educational outcomes, and reduce administrative workload.

4Smart Integrations & Expansion

Creating a comprehensive ecosystem

Goals

Expand capabilities and integrate with external systems

Key Features

  • Integration with government systems (KNEC)
  • Mobile app for parents and students
  • Library management system
  • Inventory and resource tracking
  • Multi-school management capabilities

Outcome

A comprehensive ecosystem that connects all aspects of school management with external stakeholders and supports scaling to multiple institutions.
